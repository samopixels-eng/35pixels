Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Color Dots ("the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  • Log Data: In case of an error in the App, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data to help us improve the service.
  • Device Identifiers: We may use advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) to provide personalized advertisements.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies. These services include:

  • Google AdMob: Used for providing advertisements.
    » Google Privacy & Terms
  • RevenueCat: Used for processing and validating in-app purchases.

3. In-App Purchases

All financial transactions are processed through the Google Play Store (or Apple App Store). We do not collect or store your credit card numbers, bank account information, or any other financial data. We only receive a confirmation (receipt) from the store to unlock your purchased items.

4. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Account and Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account or any personal information associated with the App, you may request deletion through the following methods:

  • Data Deletion Request Form: You can submit a formal request via our Data Deletion Request Form.
  • Email: You can also contact us directly at samopixels@gmail.com to request data removal.

For guest users (not logged in), please note that your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App cache in your device settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
